### How to Use

1. **Start with one domain** - Complete all beginner questions first
2. **Try each question** before looking at the answer (marked with ✓)
3. **Read explanations** for both correct and incorrect answers
4. **Progress to intermediate** when scoring 80%+ on beginner
5. **Tackle advanced** before taking full practice exams
6. **Mix domains** for full exam simulation

**Tip:** Cover the answer section while attempting each question to test yourself properly.

### Study Tips

- **Don't memorize answers** - Understand the concepts
- **Focus on anti-patterns** - They appear as wrong answers
- **Time yourself** - ~1.8 minutes per question
- **Track weak areas** - Review those domains more
- **Retake missed questions** - Until you get them right

### Exam Simulation

After completing all questions:
1. Take 50 random questions (timed: 90 minutes)
2. Score yourself (need 72% to pass)
3. Review all mistakes
4. Retake weak domains
5. Repeat until consistently scoring 75%+
